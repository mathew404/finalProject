About Jacob & Co.

Crafting timepieces and jewelry that redefine luxury and innovation.

Our Story

Founded in 1986 by Jacob Arabo, Jacob & Co. has become a name synonymous with innovation, artistry, and elegance in the world of luxury watches and jewelry.

From the iconic Five Time Zone Watch to groundbreaking pieces like the Astronomia, Jacob & Co. continues to revolutionize the industry.

Our Founder

Jacob Arabo’s journey began as a young apprentice in New York, honing his craft and developing a unique style that led to iconic creations.

Innovation and Craftsmanship

Jacob & Co. transforms bold ideas into reality, blending technical mastery and artistic flair.

Each piece uses the finest materials, showcasing mechanical marvels that push boundaries.

Milestones

  • 1986: Brand founded in New York City.
  • 2002: Five Time Zone Watch launched.
  • 2014: Astronomia collection debuted.
  • 2020: Bugatti Chiron Tourbillon introduced.
  • 2023: Recognized as a leader in luxury innovation.

Our Legacy

Jacob & Co. remains dedicated to creating pieces that inspire awe and admiration.

Celebrating timeless artistry and innovation that defines luxury for generations.
